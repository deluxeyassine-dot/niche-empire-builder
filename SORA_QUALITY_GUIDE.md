## WHAT OPEN-SORA IS GOOD FOR ✅

1. **Short Social Clips (2-5 seconds)**
   - TikTok intros/outros
   - Instagram Reels transitions
   - YouTube Shorts b-roll

2. **Simple Scenes**
   - Nature landscapes (forests, oceans, skies)
   - Single-subject videos (one person, one object)
   - Abstract/artistic content
   - Text overlays with motion backgrounds

3. **Non-Realistic Content**
   - Anime style
   - 3D render style
   - Artistic/painterly
   - Abstract visuals

4. **Prototyping & Drafts**
   - Test video ideas before paying for Sora
   - Create rough drafts

---

## WHAT OPEN-SORA IS NOT GOOD FOR ❌

1. **Complex Multi-Person Scenes**
   - People interacting
   - Crowds
   - Sports/action

2. **Detailed Facial Expressions**
   - Close-up faces can look uncanny
   - Lip sync doesn't work

3. **Long-Form Content**
   - Max ~5 seconds limits usefulness
   - No scene continuity

4. **Hyper-Realistic Footage**
   - Documentary-style won't work
   - News/journalism impossible

5. **Complex Physics**
   - Water splashing
   - Fabric movement
   - Collisions

### Scene: "A person walking down a city street"

**Sora 2 Result:**
- Natural gait
- Accurate reflections
- Background people move naturally
- Clothing physics correct

**Open-Sora Result:**
- Walking motion can be jerky
- Limbs may distort briefly
- Background may glitch
- Best used as quick b-roll
